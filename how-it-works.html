How Siza Works

Finding the perfect venue should be exciting — not stressful.

Siza connects people looking for venues with beautiful spaces ready to host unforgettable moments.

Whether you're planning a wedding, birthday, corporate event, Photoshoot, or celebration, Siza makes the process simple and smooth.

Step 1: Explore amazing venues

Browse through a variety of venues — gardens, halls, rooftops, creative spaces, and unique locations.

Each listing includes:

  • Photos
  • Venue details
  • Capacity information
  • Booking options

You can explore until you find the space that feels just right.

Step 2: Check availability and request a booking

Once you find a venue you like, simply request a booking directly through Siza.

Your request is sent to the venue owner so they can confirm availability and details.

Step 3: Connect with the venue owner

After your request is submitted, the venue owner reviews it and gets in touch to confirm the booking.

You can then finalize arrangements and prepare for your event.

Step 4: Make memories

Now comes the best part — hosting your event in a beautiful space and creating unforgettable moments.

From intimate gatherings to big celebrations, Siza helps you find the perfect place to bring people together.
